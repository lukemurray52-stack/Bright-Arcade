About Bright Arcade

Bright Arcade is a collection of free educational games designed to support student learning across a range of curriculum areas. The website was developed from classroom experience and aims to provide engaging, accessible and purposeful educational activities that can be used in both school and home learning environments.

Bright Arcade is not-for-profit, ad-free and designed to be simple, safe and accessible for students and teachers.

Why Bright Arcade Was Created

Bright Arcade was developed based on classroom experience as a NSW primary school teacher. The aim was to create educational games that are engaging, easy to access and focused on meaningful learning. Activities are designed to support student skill development while remaining simple for teachers to incorporate into classroom learning.
